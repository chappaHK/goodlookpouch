Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

+

Our minimum order quantity starts at 2,000 pieces for most pouch types. This allows us to maintain quality standards and provide competitive pricing.

Do you charge setup costs?

+

No, we offer zero setup costs and no cylinder charges. You only pay for the pouches you order, making us one of the most cost-effective options in the industry.

How long does production take?

+

Our standard turnaround time is 7 days for most orders. Rush orders can be accommodated with priority production scheduling.

What pouch types do you offer?

+

We specialize in Standup Pouches, Center Sealed Pouches, Zipper Pouches, Coffee Valve Pouches, and Energy Bar Pouches. We also offer custom solutions for unique requirements.

Do you provide custom printing?

+

Yes, we offer full custom printing services including multi-color designs, branding, nutritional information, and barcodes. Our design team can help create the perfect packaging for your product.

What materials do you use?

+

We use high-quality food-grade materials including various laminates, foils, and sustainable options. All materials meet industry standards for food safety and durability.
